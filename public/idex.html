COVID-19

299 confirmed new cases infected with the coronavirus (COVID-19) virus bringing the total number of positive cases in Qatar to date to 215,742. Of these positive cases, 186 are cases recorded among community members within Qatar and 113 cases among travellers returning from abroad who are subject to quarantine. All new cases are in isolation and are receiving the necessary health care according to their health status. 354 people recovered from the virus during the past 24 hours, bringing the total number of people that have recovered in Qatar to 211,296. 3 new deaths were recorded in the past 24 hours. The total number of coronavirus-related deaths to date in Qatar to date is 547. In addition, the Ministry announces 3 new deaths, aged 46, 61 and 86 years of age, all of which had a history of chronic disease. All were receiving the necessary medical care.
